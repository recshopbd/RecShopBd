Redeem Guide

Steam Keys

  • Launch the Steam Client.
  • Click the Games Menu at the top.
  • Choose Activate a Product on Steam...
  • Paste your RecShop Key.
  • The game is added Instantly.

Wallet Cards

  • Visit the Redeem Wallet page on Steam.
  • Enter your RecShop Wallet Code.
  • Click Continue to add funds.
  • Converted based on your Region.

Refund Policy

Codes are revealed upon purchase. Our policy is strict to prevent fraud.

Eligible for Refund:

  • The key provided is invalid (screenshot proof required).
  • Product is out of stock after verification.

Not Eligible:

  • Change of mind after delivery.
  • PC Hardware incompatibility.
  • Incorrect Region purchase.
  • Owning the game already.
