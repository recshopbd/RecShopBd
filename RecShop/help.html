Redeem Guide

How to redeem Steam Keys

  • Launch the Steam Client and log into your account.
  • Click the Games Menu at the top of the window.
  • Choose Activate a Product on Steam...
  • Follow the on-screen instructions and paste your RecShop Key.
  • The game will be added to your Library instantly.

How to redeem Wallet Cards

  • Visit the Steam Wallet Redeem page on your browser.
  • Enter your Wallet Code delivered by RecShop.
  • Click Continue to add the funds to your account.
  • Note: Funds will be converted based on your Store Region.

Refund Policy

At RecShop, we deal exclusively in Digital Intangible Goods. Because codes are revealed upon purchase, our refund policy is strict to prevent fraud.

Eligible for Refund/Replacement:

  • The key provided is invalid or already redeemed (requires screenshot proof).
  • The product is out of stock after payment is verified.

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Change of mind after the key has been delivered.
  • Game incompatibility with your PC hardware.
  • Incorrect region purchase by the customer.
  • Accidental purchase of a game you already own.
